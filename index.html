About the Tech Fiesta

A premiere two-day event organized by STME at NMIMS Hyderabad in February 2025, this fest brings together students from all six NMIMS campuses to celebrate technical innovation and cultural vibrancy. The event promotes interdisciplinary collaboration and provides a unique platform for showcasing exceptional student talent.

The Tech Fiesta offers an exciting mix of competitive and interactive events, including a high-stakes Hackathon, NM Sharks pitch competition, and an innovative Project Expo. Winners will receive exclusive benefits like internships, mentorship opportunities, and potential seed funding for their innovative ideas.
