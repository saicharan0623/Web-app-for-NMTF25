JALSA

Theme: Rhythm of The Regions

Get ready to witness an explosion of talent, creativity, and cultural flair at JALSA, the ultimate inter-school cultural extravaganza! Schools from across the region will showcase their best in themed performances, including dance, music, and drama, with each team captivating the audience in their 20-minute act.


The highlight of the event is the coveted "TFT 2025", awarded to the school that delivers the most outstanding performance, embodying the spirit of artistic excellence and teamwork.




How to Participate

To participate, schools must first coordinate with their respective campus cultural club and the NMTF Campus Ambassador to finalize their team. Once the team list is submitted, individual participants can register through a separate registration link, which will be provided to the selected teams.
