NM SHARKS

Prize Pool: Seed Funding from Investors
Event Date: 14th February 2025

NM Sharks is a platform where budding entrepreneurs present their innovative ideas to a panel of investors. Participants will compete for funding and mentorship, evaluated on innovation, scalability, and market potential. Teams must meet or exceed their investment target, relying on strong presentations and feasible business plans to succeed.

PPT Template

Event Format

  • Participants: Individuals or teams
  • Screening Round: Students are required to submit a video explaining their idea, along with a PowerPoint template. These submissions will be used for the shortlisting process.
  • Pitch Duration: 10 minutes, followed by 5 minutes of Q&A

Timeline

  • Abstract Submission Deadline: 30th December
  • Event Date: 14th February
  • Estimated Duration: 2–3 hours

Competition Rules

  • Participants are expected to present original ideas with clear business plans
  • Shortlisted entries from the screening round will proceed to the final pitch
  • Detailed rules and guidelines will be shared closer to the event

Participants will be evaluated based on:

  • Innovation in their business idea
  • Scalability of the proposed solution
  • Market potential
  • Feasibility of the business plan

Prize

Winners will receive investments from the panel of investors based on their business proposals.
