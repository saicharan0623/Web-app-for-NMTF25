FUTURE MINDS

Prizes Pool: GPT Subscription
Event Date: February 14-15, 2025

Future Minds: The AI Prompt Revolution is an innovative two-day event that brings together AI enthusiasts, creators, and innovators to explore the fascinating world of Generative AI and prompt engineering. Join us for an immersive experience where you'll learn to harness the power of AI tools, create sophisticated prompts, and compete in challenges that push the boundaries of AI interaction.

Event Structure

Day 1 - Workshop

  • Introduction to Generative AI and its applications
  • Prompt engineering fundamentals and best practices
  • Hands-on sessions with popular AI tools
  • Real-world case studies and examples
  • Interactive Q&A sessions

Day 2 - Competition

  • Round 1: Quick Prompt Challenge
    • Rapid-fire prompt creation
    • Time-based challenges
    • Multiple AI tools testing
  • Round 2: Advanced Problem Solving
    • Complex scenario resolution
    • Creative prompt chain development
    • Real-world application focus

General Rules

  • 1 member per team
  • All participants must attend both days
  • Bring your fully charged laptops
  • Original work only - no plagiarism
  • Judges' decisions are final

Competition Guidelines

  • Time limits must be strictly followed
  • Only approved AI tools can be used
  • Solutions must be documented
  • Teams must present their approach

Event Resources

  • Workshop Materials:

    • Practice scenarios
    • On-site technical assistance
    • Troubleshooting help
